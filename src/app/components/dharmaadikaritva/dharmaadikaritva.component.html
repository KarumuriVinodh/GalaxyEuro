MOOWR:
Manufacture and Other Operations in Warehouse Regulations or MOOWR was first mooted in 1962 under Section 65 of the Customs Act by the Government of India. The procedure, documentation and compliances were streamlined in2019 and called MOOWR 2019. We are the only Consultancy with the expertise to handle this in Southern India.
